About James David O'Neill

James David O'Neill is a software developer working for CloudCIX Limited. James works in Cloud automation for both Linux and Windows servers. His language of choice is Python, and works with the django REST frameworks to design APIs. James is currently studying a BSc in Physics and Mathematical Sciences at University College Cork, Ireland. In the past James has worked as a physics and programming tutor for the University.
In his spare time, James plays guitar, sings and is vice chairperson of the UCC Physics and Astronomy Society.
